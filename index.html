Linkin Park

img

An American rock band from mount agora, California. The band was founded in 1996, and their debut album Hybrid Theory (2000) gained international fame, was certified as diamond by the RIAA in 2005 and has released multiple platinum albums in several other countries. The next studio album, Meteora, continued the band's success, topping the Billboard 200 album chart in 2003, followed by extensive touring and charity events
